The purpose of this site is to promote the company Making Waves, as well as direct customers to specific trips, handle scheduling, provide information on trips.

  • Experienced rafters
  • New rafters
  • Scenarios

  • What is the pricing for group sizes, length of the trip, and which routes they take?
  • Are there any package deals?
  • Is there any lodging nearby?
